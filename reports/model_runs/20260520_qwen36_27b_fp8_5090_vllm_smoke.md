## Decision

- Decision label: diagnostic-only proceed for bounded 4k FP8 27B experiments.
- The artifact is deployable on the RTX 5090 for short 4k, single-request text generation if `gpu_memory_utilization=0.98`, no CPU offload, `language_model_only=True`, `skip_mm_profiling=True`, and chat-template thinking is disabled.
- It is not yet approved for reviewed-gold benchmark scoring. The memory headroom is narrow, first-request JIT warnings remain, `enforce_eager=True` disables compile/CUDA graphs, and 8k/32k or batch behavior has not been tested.
- The new-company benchmark work should still proceed through reviewed context/facts and exact-value ledger gates before any 20-company scored inference claim.

## Safety Notes

- No SSH password, token, or temporary credential is stored in this report.

- Not run: 8k/32k context, 4bit model, SEC benchmark prompt, BGE-M3/Judgment Plan integration, batch throughput, or long-output synthesis.
