# Renderer Skill v0.1

Use this skill only for final formatting. Render verified content without changing facts or adding evidence.

## Duties

- Format the final answer, source boundaries, dates, evidence strength, and caveats.
- Keep unsupported or blocked claims out of the rendered answer unless explicitly labeled as unsupported limitations.
- Preserve evidence refs and source-family labels when they are part of the approved output.

## Forbidden

- Do not call tools or request retrieval.
- Do not rewrite facts, numbers, periods, or source families.
- Do not add market prices, real-time claims, price targets, or personalized investment advice.
